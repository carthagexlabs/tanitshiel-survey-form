# TanitShield — Credential Verification Pain Point Survey

A research survey form built by **CarthageX Labs** to understand the challenges companies face with cross-border credential verification. The insights gathered directly inform the development of [TanitShield](https://carthagexlabs.xyz).

## Live Survey

[https://carthagexlabs-tanitshield.netlify.app/](https://carthagexlabs-tanitshield.netlify.app/)

## Overview

This survey targets HR leaders, recruiters, and compliance professionals who deal with international candidate credential verification. It collects data on:

- Company profile and role
- Current verification processes and timelines
- Key pain points and friction areas
- Openness to blockchain/ZK-proof-based solutions
- Interest in a pilot programme

## Privacy

Responses are confidential and are not shared with third parties. Data processing inquiries can be directed to `privacy@carthagexlabs.xyz`.
